# AI Website Copy Generator – Task 1

## Business Selected

Urban Bites Café – Durban

## Objective

The objective of this project was to design a structured prompt system capable of generating high-quality, conversion-focused website copy for a local business through the use of artificial intelligence tools. The scope of the generated content included homepage copy, services page content, and call-to-action sections.

## Tools Used

- ChatGPT for prompt engineering and content generation
- GitHub for documentation and submission purposes

## Prompt Strategy

A three-stage structured prompt framework was developed to guide the content generation process. The first stage, the Master Prompt, was designed to produce the initial draft of the website content, covering the homepage, services, and call-to-action sections. The second stage, the Improvement Prompt, focused on refining this output by enhancing emotional appeal, strengthening the value proposition, and integrating local search engine optimisation keywords such as "Durban café" and "affordable food Durban". The third stage, the Final Refinement Prompt, aimed to improve overall clarity and readability through sentence shortening, increased impact, and optimisation of call-to-action microcopy to support higher conversion rates.

## Output Summary

The artificial intelligence system generated a complete set of website copy tailored for a café in Durban targeting students and young professionals. The final output included a strong value proposition, clear service descriptions, high-converting call-to-action sections, and content optimised for local search engine visibility.

## Improvements Made

Several improvements were implemented during the refinement stages. Long paragraphs were converted into shorter, more readable sections. Headlines and value statements were strengthened to increase engagement. A sense of urgency was introduced into call-to-action phrasing through elements such as "takes 30 seconds" and "don't wait". The overall tone was adjusted to sound more natural and engaging, while relevant local search engine optimisation keywords were embedded throughout the copy.

## Key Learning

This project demonstrates the capacity of prompt engineering to serve as an alternative to traditional copywriting workflows. Through structured prompting, artificial intelligence tools can generate realistic, client-ready website content that supports improved business communication and conversion outcomes.

## Conclusion

The final output produced through this process is realistic, professional, and suitable for implementation by an operational business. The project illustrates the potential of structured prompting techniques to address real-world content requirements for local enterprises.
